Solutions

    option 1

  • Change Video Quality

    A pop-up message prompts users to adjust the video quality and lower the resolution, ensuring smooth playback.

    option 2

  • Loading Spinner

    A loading spinner designed in a similar style to the game, uses repetitive loading animations to reduce the perception of wait time.

    option 3 [Backlog]

  • 2D Virtual Dealer

    Users can switch from high-quality videos to a virtual character, which require less data, allowing for faster loading and playback, maintaining the smoothness and playability of the game.

This confirmed that our UX fixes were driving real impact.

Through thoughtful loading screen design and adaptive solutions, we turned frustration into engagement. And prove that small UX changes can drive big results.
