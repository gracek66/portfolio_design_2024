GRACE KWONG

Infographic for Airport Authority

Hong Kong International Airport (HKIA) strives to become a “smart airport” by leveraging innovative technology to provide convenient services for passengers.

Passengers can use the self-bag drop service to check their luggage upon arrival at HKIA. In the future, “My Flight App” will not only showflight information, but also notify passengers the boarding time and upon baggage arrival. Departing and Arriving passengers will enjoy more efficient and comfortable services at HKIA.
